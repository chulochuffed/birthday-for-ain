Happy Birthday Ain Alisha

Every star tonight carries a quiet wish for you.

for Ain Alisha

I.

What the Stars Keep

Time moves the way starlight does—
slow, and already far behind itself,
arriving here only to say:
you made it this far.

Somewhere a small light bends
across a thousand quiet years
just to land, tonight, on you—
proof that waiting can still be kind,
that hope is only patience
wearing a brighter coat.

So let the hours be gentle.
Let tomorrow arrive unhurried.
The sky has kept your name
in every constellation it has drawn.

II.

A Garden, Half-Dreamed

Somewhere your happiness is a garden—
not yet in bloom, only beginning,
roots reaching quietly
toward everything still unwritten.

May your dreams grow like wild petals,
unbothered by the shape
anyone expected them to take.
May softness find you often,
and joy arrive
in the smallest, simplest rooms.

Bloom slowly. Bloom sideways.
Bloom in your own good season.
The world can wait
for a happiness this honest.

May this year be kinder to you than the last.

Happy Birthday,
Ain Alisha.
